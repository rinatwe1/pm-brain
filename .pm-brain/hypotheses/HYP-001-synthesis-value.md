# HYP-001: Synthesis Value Hypothesis

## Hypothesis
We believe that PMs who use /synthesize-meeting will feel that Claude gives them significantly better product assistance — because context is richer and connected. We will measure by: PM reports "Claude reminded me of something relevant I would have missed."

## Test Method
Use PM Brain on PM Brain for 30 days. Track: how many times did a synthesis lead to a decision or hypothesis being surfaced proactively?

## Definition of Validated
At least 3 instances where synthesis output directly influenced a product decision within 30 days.

## Definition of Invalidated
After 30 days, synthesis artifacts sit unused and don't influence decisions.
